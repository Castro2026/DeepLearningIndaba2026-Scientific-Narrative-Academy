## The method in 30 seconds

**Read (3-Pass):** ① scan 5–10 min → decide · ② grasp ≤ 1 h → summarize to a colleague · ③ reconstruct 1–5 h → critique & new ideas.
**Write (Blueprint):** ① Frame — title & funnel introduction · ② Core — re-implementable method + FAIR data · ③ Impact — honest, labeled figures, mean ± std over seeds · ④ Honesty — specific limitations.

## The 6-month challenge

Your first submitted paper within six months — a workshop (AfricaNLP, Indaba) counts.
Weekly routine: 3 papers in Pass 1, 1 in Pass 2, 1/month in Pass 3 (~90 min/week).
